About Us

We opened the doors of the first Company of Cars retail location in 2008 after more than ten years mixing it up in the hyper competitive automotive wholesale business. Originally located on the corner of Broadway and Quebec just outside downtown Vancouver, we moved our store in 2014 to a new larger space at 1502 Boundary Road on the Burnaby / Vancouver border. For some of our loyal Vancouver customers that was a bit too far. We found a better location back in the heart of Vancouver at 1885 Clark Drive near 1st Ave to better serve our customers.
